ABOUT US

Who are we?

Uniting PJ for a Better Community. Join us in fostering connections, embracing diversity, and driving positive change in Petaling Jaya. Our vibrant club offers social events, cultural festivals, sports activities, and educational workshops for all ages. Together, we strive to enhance the quality of life in PJ through collaboration with local authorities, businesses, and non-profit organizations. Explore our website for upcoming events, membership benefits, and community projects. Be part of Kelab PJ and make a difference in our beloved city. Together, we can create a stronger, more inclusive PJ for everyone.
